About Me

Gary Gardner

Hello, my name is Gary Gardner and I live in Sierra Vista, Arizona. I love Mexican, Asia, and Italian food. I really like to watch old television from the 50s to the 90s. Such as MASH, Waltons, and Happy Days. I also like to visit with my family such as my wife, children, parents, siblings, and now my two grandsons.

I have been working with computers ever since I used my first one at Ricks College in 1980, After graduation at Ricks College, I served a full-time mission in Idaho. I grew up in Torrance California, and Idaho was pure culture and climate shock to a young surfer.

Most of my career was spent working as a technical trainer, certified Microsoft professional, computer security specialist, and Cisco admin. I had a stroke in 2002 when I was 42. I am disabled and can only type with one hand, college has been a bit of a challenge.

I have been a member of the Church of Jesus Christ all my life. My favorite quote is by President Kimball, DO IT NOW! My favorite scripture is 1 Nephi 3:7 And it came to pass that I, Nephi, said unto my father: I will go and do the things which the Lord hath commanded, for I know that the Lord giveth no commandments unto the children of men, save he shall prepare a way for them that they may accomplish the thing which he commandeth them.
